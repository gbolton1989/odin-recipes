Lasagna

Description

This recipe is for lasagna. My grandmother never cooked this kind of stuff but I still loved it. I just had to learn how to make it myself one day. So here is my recipe.

Ingredients

  • Meat sauce
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds mozarella cheese
  1. Put pasta water on to boil.
  2. Boil and drain noodles.
  3. Heat oven to 375
  4. Assemble Lasagna
  5. Bake for 45 minutes
  6. Cool and serve
